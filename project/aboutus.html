Youth Association "STARt" Hadžići

When and why we were created?

At the founding session in Hadžići in July 2023, the first youth association in Hadžići since the establishment of the municipality was formed. A group of confused young people, eager for knowledge and space in the local community, created the association. Twelve members attended the founding session, from all parts of Hadžići. From that first day, it was evident that there was a good energy and self-confidence among the youth.

What's our goal?

The goal of our association is to promote activism among youth, support their engagement in social and environmental issues, and create positive changes in our local community. Through various projects, campaigns, and actions, we aim to improve the living conditions of young people by providing opportunities for skills development, education, and participation in social processes. Simultaneously, we are dedicated to enhancing the environmental situation through education, promoting sustainable practices, and actively participating in environmental protection initiatives. Through our work, we aim to inspire young people to take responsibility for their community and environment and contribute to building a better, more just, and environmentally conscious future.

Cooperation

Partners play a crucial role in achieving our association's goals and mission. Their support enables us to implement diverse projects and activities that have a positive impact on the community and young people. Through their engagement, we gain access to resources, knowledge, and networks that help us make a greater impact and achieve lasting change. Thanks to our partners, we have the opportunity to create sustainable projects and initiatives that contribute to a better future for all.
